# 🧭 Capiverse

> An AI-powered tourism platform for Cape Town — helping travellers discover tours, attractions, and personalised itineraries.

**[Live Demo](https://capeverse.vercel.app)**

---

## Overview

Capiverse is a full-stack tourism platform built for Cape Town. Visitors can explore curated tours and attractions, generate personalised day-by-day itineraries based on their travel style, group type, interests, and budget — powered by OpenAI. Tour operators and admins manage all content through a protected dashboard with full CRUD capabilities and enquiry management.

---

## Features

- 🗺️ **Tour & attraction listings** — Curated Cape Town experiences with slugged detail pages
- 🤖 **AI trip planner** — GPT-4o-mini generates personalised multi-day itineraries based on travel style, pace, group type, budget & interests
- 📊 **Attraction scoring engine** — Custom ranking algorithm matches attractions to traveller preferences before passing to AI
- 📅 **Itinerary builder** — Day-by-day planner with pace-aware stop grouping (Relaxed / Moderate / Packed) and budget band estimates
- 💬 **AI assistant** — Conversational travel assistant for Cape Town questions
- 📬 **Enquiry system** — Contact form with nodemailer email delivery and admin inbox
- 🔒 **Admin dashboard** — Protected CMS for managing tours, attractions and enquiries with rate limiting
- 🧪 **Test suite** — Jest unit tests covering auth, mail, rate limiting, schemas, scoring & utils
- 🐳 **Docker support** — Dockerfile and docker-compose included for containerised deployment
